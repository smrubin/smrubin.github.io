About A Litte More About Me

I'm a technology consultant, specializing in front-end web design and development. I'm passionate about creating a seamless and responsive user experience, staying up-to-date on the latest web technologies, writing code that can be understood and modified by anyone, and most importantly, improving business through technology.

In my current role at the Deloitte Digital Federal Practice, I work in an agile environment with federal agency clients to design and develop the front-end for a suite of business applications. Responsibilities include but are not limited to: rapid prototyping of new application features; integrating front-end code with back-end systems; using COTS tools' APIs to access data; full design and development of application suite portal and landing page; writing custom JavaScript for COTS applications; and collaborating with functional team to develop front-end user stories and project deliverables.

I've worked with a lot of technologies in my career, but am currently excited about Angular, jQuery, Bootstrap, and Git.

I graduated from the University of Virginia's School of Engineering and Applied Science in May 2014. I received a B.S. in Systems Engineering with High Honors, and a minor in business.
